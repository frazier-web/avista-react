President & Founder
Danny Gonzalez
As President and Founder of Avista Real Estate Group, Danny leads a team dedicated to representing buyers and sellers of prestigious properties in Mazatlán and along the Sinaloa coast. As a life-long Mazatleco he posses in-depth knowledge of the local market and an extensive network of contacts making him an indispensable resource in the city’s most exclusive neighborhoods.

With years of experience in major transactions in Cerritos, La Marina, Sábalo, Gaviotas, and beyond, Danny has delivered record-breaking results with a client-centered approach that defines Avista’s reputation.
